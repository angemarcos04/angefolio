## Overview

Aulert is an early campus concern reporting and triage concept. It asks where AI can reduce the work of organizing incoming reports without turning an uncertain model into an unquestioned decision-maker.

## Problem

Campus concerns may arrive through different channels with uneven detail and no shared priority system. Staff still need enough context to route each concern responsibly.

## Approach

The prototype direction combines structured intake with AI-assisted classification, summarization, priority scoring, and routing suggestions. Every generated signal is intended to support human review rather than replace it.

## Current status

Aulert is a prototype-stage concept. The workflow and boundaries are being planned; the AI behavior, interface, and backend are not presented as production-ready.

## What I am learning

- Designing AI features around uncertainty and human review
- Separating useful automation from decisions that need accountability
- Planning data and API boundaries before committing to a model provider
